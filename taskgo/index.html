FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Can't find an answer? Reach our team at hello@taskgo.io.

How is TaskGo different from Jira or Asana?

TaskGo is built keyboard-first and offline-first. We focus on speed, beautiful defaults and AI-driven summaries — instead of bolting on features for every edge case.

Do you offer a free plan?

Yes. Our Starter plan is free forever for teams up to 10 people, with unlimited tasks and projects.

Can I migrate from another tool?

One-click importers are available for Jira, Trello, Asana, Linear, Notion and 9 more. Your structure, comments and history come along for the ride.

Is my data secure?

TaskGo is SOC 2 Type II certified, encrypts data in transit and at rest, and offers SSO/SCIM on all paid tiers. Custom data residency is available on Enterprise.

Do you have a mobile app?

Yes — native iOS and Android apps, both with offline support and capture-on-the-go.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. There are no contracts on Starter or Pro. Cancel from the billing page in two clicks.
